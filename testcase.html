Bali

  • Bali is a sun-soaked paradise that attracts every type of traveler, from budget-conscious backpackers to luxury jet-setters.
  • Bali is the only island in Indonesia that is primarily Hindu and it is an excellent hub of culture and adventure.

Goa

  • Goa is known for its scenic beaches like Calangute, Baga, Candolim, Colva, Agonda, and Palolem.
  • Goa is home to several historical sites such as the Basilica of Bom Jesus, which was established in 1605 and is one of the most popular tourist attractions.
  • Goa offers a unique cultural experience with its flea markets, food, and festivals. The Saturday Night Market is a must-visit.
